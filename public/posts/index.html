Astro and Comets

2024-10-21: Finally the comet was seen!

The maximum brightness was missed due to cloudy weather. At a magnitude of 4.4 the observation was difficult to the naked eyes. Dark-adaption and averted-vision helped a lot, and of course the optical tools:

  • 8x20 Trinovid binocular
  • 10" Dobsonian with 2" wide-angle Erfle-eyepiece, two degree field-of-view
  • DSLR on a tripod, using a fixed focal length 50mm-Planar (f/1,7), which acts as 75mm due to CropFactor.
